# Kitchen Refresh

**Status:** Planning
**Goal:** Update kitchen without a full renovation — new cabinet paint, hardware, backsplash, and light fixture

## Scope

This is a cosmetic refresh only, no structural changes. Targeting a more modern look — current kitchen has honey oak cabinets and dated tile backsplash.

## Plan

- **Cabinet paint:** Sherwin-Williams Alabaster (SW 7008) or Benjamin Moore White Dove — getting paint samples
- **Hardware:** Matte black bar pulls, 5" (replacing brass knobs) — ~$180 for all cabinets
- **Backsplash:** Remove existing 4" ceramic tile; replace with white subway tile in a stacked vertical pattern
- **Light fixture:** Replace fluorescent box with 3-pendant island fixture (black/brass finish)
