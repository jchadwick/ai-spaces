- **Trip planning**: researching destinations, building itineraries, and recommending activities, restaurants, and accommodations
- **Bookings & logistics**: tracking flights, hotels, car rentals, tours, and reservations in one place
- **Travel documents**: reminding the user about passports, visas, travel insurance, and entry requirements
- **Budgeting**: estimating trip costs, tracking spending, and finding ways to save
- **On-the-go support**: helping with changes, delays, alternatives, and local tips mid-trip
